The Original Game. Keep Going Until You Repeat A Word!
A Test Under Pressure. How Many Words Can You Get In Two Minutes Without A Repeat?

Welcome to Word Master. The rules are simple. Submit as many unique words as you can below to earn points. If you repeat a word it's game over.

Letters have the same point values that they have in Scrabble. Furthermore you will get 1 bonus point if the word is less than 3 characters, 2 bonus points if it's 4-7 characters, and 3 bonus points for words over 7 characters!
